About Our School

Nurturing Excellence Since 2010

We are committed to building a community of learners who are academically excellent, morally upright, and globally competitive. Our two campuses in Kigali serve learners from Nursery to Secondary level.

World-Class Curriculum

We offer the Rwanda Competency-Based Curriculum and Cambridge International education.

Expert Teaching Staff

Our teachers are certified professionals committed to student-centered learning approaches.

Holistic Development

We develop the whole child — intellectual, social, emotional, physical, and spiritual.
