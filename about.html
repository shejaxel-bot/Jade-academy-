Who We Are

Our Story

Excel Academy was founded in 2010 with a single, unwavering conviction: that every child in Rwanda deserves access to world-class education. Starting with just two classrooms and a handful of dedicated teachers, we have grown into one of Kigali's most respected educational institutions, serving over 1,800 learners across two campuses.

Our school sits at the intersection of Rwanda's Competency-Based Curriculum and the globally recognised Cambridge International framework — giving our students the best of both worlds: national roots and international reach.

Today, our alumni are at leading universities across Africa, Europe, and beyond — a testament to the quality of education we deliver every single day.
